Welcome to Cricket Game with Computer

Game Rules:

you can choose either Bat, Ball or Stump.
The computer will also make a choice.
The rules are as follows:

  • If you and computer choose the same option, it's a Tie.
  • If you choose Bat and computer chooses Ball, You Win!
  • If you choose Ball and computer chooses Stump, You Win!
  • If you choose Stump and computer chooses Bat, You Win!
  • In all other cases, Computer Wins! or You losses
